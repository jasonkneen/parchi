Privacy Policy

Parchi stores all settings, API keys, and conversation history locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. We never send your API keys to our servers.

When using prepaid credits, your requests are proxied through our Convex backend. We store your email address and credit balance for billing purposes. Request content is forwarded to the upstream AI provider and is not stored on our servers.

When using BYOK mode (bring your own key), requests go directly from your browser to the AI provider. No data passes through our servers.

We use Stripe for payment processing. We do not store credit card information.
